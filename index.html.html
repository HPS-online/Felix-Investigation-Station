🍎 Educator Setup Notes

  • Let him freely choose his investigation — autonomy reduces anxiety
  • Set a gentle deadline: "share when you're ready"
  • Praise the process, not just the product
  • Allow movement breaks between work sessions
  • Recording removes the pressure of live speaking — great option
  • Pre-load safe Chromebook bookmarks for each topic
  • Dinosaur expertise is a superpower — lean into it!
  • Quiz Maker puts him in the expert/teacher role — powerful!
  • Step-by-step modal reduces the overwhelm of open tasks
  • Celebrate presenting to the class as a special event
  • Consider a "wonder wall" for posting new questions to investigate
  • Cross-topic projects work well for extended engagement
